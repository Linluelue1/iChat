剑桥雅思12Test7Section1听力答案解析 Public Library

剑桥雅思12 Test7 Section1听力答案解析
第1题答案：travel
对应原文：we’ve got a much larger section of the library devoted to
travel
books now, so you should come and have a look.
答案解析：听到section意识到答案即将到来。题目采用简单的语序颠倒的手法，将原文位于空前的books放在了答案之后，有点考验大家的短期记忆能力和对短语的认知。
第2题答案：history
对应原文：actually we’ve now got a new section with materials on the
history
of the town and surrounding region.
答案解析：听到new section意识到答案即将到来。根据介词on判断答案为history，也可以通过town and surrounding region与local的同义替换来确定答案。
第3题答案：study
对应原文：
It can be hired out for meetings.
But at other times people can use it to
study
答案解析：听到meetings意识到答案即将到来，由词性判断答案为study。
第4题答案：teenagers
对应原文：We’ve introduced a special section of fiction written specially for
teenagers
, but they might be a bit young for that
答案解析：这道题没有明显的提示词，不过根据题干我们可以事先猜测出空上应该填某种人，由此锁定teenagers。
第5题答案：kitchen
对应原文：At the next meeting, they’re going to be doing experiments with stuff that everyone has in the
kitchen
– sugar and
flour
and so on
答案解析：听到experiment意识到答案即将到来，从题干可以推测空上应该填的是某个地方，由此锁定kitchen。
第6题答案：crime
对应原文：It’s called “Catch the Mouse” and she based the story on a
crime
that actually took place here years ago
答案解析：根据novel与story的同义替换定位到这句话，空前介词on没有任何改变，由此确定答案为crime。
第7题答案：appointment/booking
对应原文：It’s on Tuesday mornings – they don’t need to make an
appointment
or anything, they just turn up.
答案解析：听到Tuesday意识到答案即将到来。do not need与no … necessary对应，由此确定答案为appointment为答案。
第8题答案：sugar
对应原文：The hospital arranges for someone to come along and measure the level of
sugar
in your blood
答案解析：同样是采用语序颠倒的手法，将原本位于空前的blood放在了答案之后，考察大家的短期记忆能力。
第9题答案：stamps
对应原文：
we do have a little shop with things like wall charts and greetings cards, and also
stamps
,
so you can post the cards straightaway, which is really useful.
答案解析：没有设置什么障碍，只是简单的考察并列，找到答案很容易，主要难度在于stamps的拼写。
第10题答案：parking
对应原文：
PAUL:
I’ll be bringing the car, is there
parking
available?
SUSIE:
Yes. and it’s free in the evening and at weekends.
答案解析：男性先提出询问，女性再给予肯定，也算是Section1中十分常见考察方式。
