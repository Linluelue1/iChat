剑桥雅思12 Test7 Section2听力答案解析
第11题答案：D
对应原文： there’s a noticeable growth in the number of holidays taken by the 55 to 64-year-olds
答案解析：听到55-64 years old对应D选项，同一句话中growth与题干中increasing同义替换，符合限定条件，由此判断答案为D。
第12题答案：E
对应原文：
In terms of age groups, the over-65s are an important market,
and one that’s increasing steadily year on year
答案解析：几乎没有什么难度，选项over 65原词出现，题干中的increasing也没有进行任何替换，可以很轻易确定E为正确答案。
第13题答案：A
对应原文：
but clients often say that more than this.
It’s the chance to create lasting relationships with other like-minded people that’s the main draw
答案解析：听到but意识到答案即将到来，A选项将录音中比较文艺的说法create lasting relationships with other like-minded people替换为make new friends，由此锁定答案。
第14题答案：C
对应原文：
But they do often take place outside the main tourist centres,
which gives an opportunity for clients to find out more about the local people and customs
答案解析：听到but转折意识到后面跟的是正确答案，local people and customs对应different culture，由此确定C为正确答案。
第15题答案：C
对应原文：
We’ve got an excellent team of artists to lead the classes – some of them have been with us from the start.
And five additional ones will be joining us this year
答案解析：录音中提到，今年将会增加5名艺术家，由此确定C为正确答案。假期的举办地址一直都是4个，由此排除A。而B选项则完全没有提到。
第16题答案：B
对应原文：But we focus on dishes from a great many different ones
答案解析：原文中提到，他们的菜肴来自许多不同的国家，对应B选项international focus，由此确定答案。A选项错在only，而C选项在错在mainly。
第17题答案：A
对应原文：Groups are small, no more than eight, so clients can have one-on-one tuition during the holiday
答案解析：A选项individual与原文中one-on-one同义替换，由此确定答案。录音中只是说会配备经过培训的导游，而非导师本身是导游，因此排除B。而C选项则错在selling photographs上。
第18题答案：stress
对应原文：
As well as improving general fitness, clients find that they end up losing much of the
stress
they’ve built up in their daily lives
答案解析：根据as well as与also的对应意识到答案即将到来，空前词reduce与losing同义替换，根据修饰关系确定答案为stress
第19题答案：weight
对应原文：In Greece, we have a two-week holiday for clients who want to do something about their
weight
答案解析：听到greece意识到答案即将到来，虽然control的同义替换不太好确定，但我们仍然可以根据词性锁定weight.
第20题答案：families
对应原文：We offer one which is tailored to the needs of
families
, which is particularly popular
答案解析：这道题主要考察tailored to与specially designed的同义替换。不过哪怕我们不认识tailored，也可以事先根据题干猜测空上应该填某一群体，由此锁定families。
