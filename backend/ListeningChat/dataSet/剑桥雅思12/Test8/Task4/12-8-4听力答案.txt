第31题答案：garden(s)
对应原文：
So they ignore important issues such as the noise someone might hear from the open windows or
gardens
of their neighbours
答案解析：听到windows意识到答案即将到来，虽然听力原文刻意打乱了语序，但根据词性和语义还是能够比较轻易的确定gardens为答案的。
第32题答案：political
对应原文：But anyway, even though these noise maps are fairly
crude
, they’ve been useful in providing information and raising awareness that noise matters, we need to deal with it and so it’s a
political
matter
答案解析：听到but anyway，意识到上一点已经结束，录音进行到了32题，空后词issue与原文中的matters同义替换，根据修饰关系，确定答案为political。
第33题答案：work/study
对应原文：And there are other problems as well, for
instance
if schoolchildren don’t have a quiet place to
study
, their
work
will suffer
答案解析：听到school children意识到答案即将到来，录音原文将题干中的of关系替换为their，由此确定答案为work。
第34题答案：fountain
对应原文： Some types of sounds that most people would probably think of as nice and relaxing might well score quite highly in decibel levels – think of the sound made by a
fountain
in a town square, for example.
答案解析：根据nice and relaxing与pleasant的同义替换锁定答案所在句子。原文采用语序颠倒的手法，将原本位于空前的e.g.放在了答案之后，稍微有点考验大家的短期记忆能力。
第35题答案：social
对应原文：Instead, many researchers are using
social
science techniques, studying people’s emotional response to sound by using questionnaires and so on.
答案解析：听到researchers意识到答案即将到来，空后词sciences在录音中原词出现，根据修饰关系确定答案为social。
第36题答案：lively
对应原文：a city needs to have a sense of activity so it needs to be
lively

clack

答案解析：听到activity意识到答案即将到来，从题干语法可以推测出空上应该要填一个形容词，筛选之后只有lively填上去语义合适，由此锁定答案。
第37题答案：training
对应原文：Apart from studying the basics of acoustics, these people receive very little
training
in this area
答案解析：空前词not much与原文中very little同义替换，根据修饰关系确定training为正确答案。
第38题答案：culture

答案解析：这道题比较简单一些，只用根据relates与relatioship的词性替换，即可轻松锁定culture。
第39题答案：nature
对应原文：Can we learn anything from physics about the

答案解析：听到physics意识到答案即将到来，题干中的所有格关系在录音中没有任何变化，可以很容易锁定nature为答案。
