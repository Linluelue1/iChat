第31题答案：creativity
对应原文：The result showed that those who chose to dance showed much more
creativity
答案解析：这道题的难点主要在于离音频开头太远，让人怀疑答案是否已经过去。不过只要能跟上节奏，还是很容易根据increases和more的对应锁定creativity的。
第32题答案：therapy
对应原文：a very useful way to help people suffering from mental health problems. He thinks dance should be prescribed as
therapy
to help people overcome issues such as depression.
答案解析：mental health提示大家答案即将到来，空前介词as在录音中原词出现，根据修饰关系确定therapy为正确答案。
第33题答案：fitness
对应原文：Anyone can have a go, even those whose standard of
fitness
is quite low
答案解析：采用语序颠倒的手法，将原本位于空前的提示词low放在了答案之后，稍微有些考验大家的短期记忆能力。
第34题答案：balance
对应原文：dancing lowers the risk of falls, which could result in a broken hip, for example, by helping people to improve their
balance
答案解析：空前词better与improve同义替换，根据短语关系，确定balance为正确答案。
第35题答案：brain
对应原文：For older people, this kind of activity is especially important because it forces their
brain
to process things more quickly and to retain more information.
答案解析：这道题没有任何提示词，只能先根据词性和句义筛选出brain，再依据后面more quickly与faster的对应确定brain就是答案。
第36题答案：motivation
对应原文：Participants report feeling less tired and having greater
motivation
to be more active
答案解析：greater与more同义替换，再加上从题干可以老烤鸭雅思看出空上应该填一个名词，由此锁定motivation为正确答案。
第37题答案：isolation
对应原文：This helps to develop new relationships and can reduce older people’s sense of
isolation
答案解析：lessen与reduce对应，feeling与sense对应，根据短语搭配和所有格，确定isolation为正确答案。
第38题答案：calories
对应原文：a class lasting 40 minutes burns about 370
calories
答案解析：40 minutes提示大家答案即将到来，many对应具体数字370，由此确定答案为calories。
第39题答案：obesity
对应原文： when women with
obesity
did Zumba three times a week for 16 weeks
答案解析：women提示大家答案即将到来，从空前词suffering from可以推测出空上应该填某种不好的状态，由此确定obesity为正确答案。
第40题答案：habit
对应原文：More importantly, the women enjoyed the class so much that they made it a
habit
and continued to attend classes at least once a week
答案解析：became与made的同义替换不太好识别，还是根据词性和句义对重读词汇进行筛选比较容易一些。
