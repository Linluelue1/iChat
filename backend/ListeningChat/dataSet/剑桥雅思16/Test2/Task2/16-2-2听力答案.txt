第11题答案：C
对应原文：Neville Richards, and intended as his family home, but he died before it was completed. His heir chose to sell it to the local council, who turned it into offices. A later plan to convert it into a tourist information centre didn’t come about
答案解析：三个选项在原文中都有提到。商人想把它当作自己的家，但完成之前就去世了。随后它被卖给当地委员会作为自己的办公室。虽然有计划将它改造成游客信息中心，但并未实现。由此排除A和B，确定C为正确答案。
第12题答案：B
对应原文：The lower school will move to new buildings that will be constructed on the main site. Developers will construct houses on the existing lower school site
答案解析：Lower school会搬到主校区新建成的建筑里，而其原址上老烤鸭雅思会建起新的房屋。A选项中的all属于无中生有，而C选项则错在了additional school buildings（是houses）。B选项new homes对应construct houses，由此确定为正确答案。
第13题答案：A
对应原文：But there’s only one serving point, so most pupils have to wait a considerable time to be served
答案解析：这道题比较简单一些，B选项的changes和C选项的dissatisfaction均未提到。A选项long queuing times对应wait a considerable time，由此确定为正确答案。
第14题答案：A
对应原文：but one thing we ask you to do, to help in the smooth running of the Food Hall, is to discuss with your children each morning which type of food they want to eat that day, this article is from Laokaoya website, so they can go straight to the relevant serving point.
答案解析：家长需要做的事情是提前跟自己的孩子商量好吃什么，以便他们可以直接前往相应的窗口，由此确定A为正确答案。B选项的money和C选项的healthy food在原文中也有提到，但并不是家长要做的。
第15题答案：C
对应原文：We’ll still have tables and chairs in there, and pupils can eat food from the Food Hall or lunch they’ve brought from home
答案解析：老餐厅仍然会有桌子和椅子，学生可以在那里吃东西。由此确定C为答案。
第16题答案：D
对应原文：One serving point we call World Adventures. This will serve a different country’s cuisine each day
答案解析：World Adventures每天提供不同国家的事物，即每天菜单都会变，由此确定D为正确答案。
第17题答案：A
对应原文：We’ll ask pupils to make suggestions
答案解析：Street life会邀请学生提意见，选项中只有A能与此对应，由此确定为正确答案。
第18题答案：B
对应原文：The Speedy Italian serving point will cater particularly for the many pupils who don’t eat meat or fish
答案解析：Speedy Italian专为那些不吃肉或者不吃鱼的学生服务，对应B选项only vegetarian food，由此确定它为正确答案。
第19题答案：B
对应原文：And a former pupil has given a magnificent grand piano to the school, so a few pupils will be able to learn at the school instead of going to the local college
答案解析：这道题的关键在于题干中的new，A，D，E三个选项在原文中都有提到，但都是已经开设的课程。因此统统排除。piona这里有will表示未来，因此确定它为正确答案。
第20题答案：C
对应原文：As you know, we have a well-equipped performance centre, and we’re going to start drama classes in there
答案解析：跟上一题类似，be going to表未来，由此确定C为正确答案。
