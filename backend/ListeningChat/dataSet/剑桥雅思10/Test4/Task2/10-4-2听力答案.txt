剑桥雅思10 Test4 Section2听力答案解析
第11题答案：B
对应原文：and developed here because it’s the highest navigable point of the Avon—boats can go no higher up this river—
and proved a handy place to load and unload
cargo
to and from the sea
答案解析：录音中提到，这个地点装卸货很方便，其中handy和B选项的convenient同义替换，由此确定答案。A选项完全没有提到，C选项错在on the sea coast。
第12题答案：B
对应原文：about 900 years ago,
tin
was discovered nearby, though
it wasn’t until the Industrial Revolution, when a
tremendous
need for metals of all kinds developed
答案解析：原文中虽然确实提到了tin，但题干上有industrial revolution这一限定条件，因此排除C选B。
第13题答案：A
对应原文：
there was not enough coal in the local area
, so the rocks containing minerals had to be shipped long distances
答案解析：B选项和C选项都完全没有提到，A选项中shortage与not enough同义替换，fuel与coal同义替换，由此确定答案。
第14题答案：A
对应原文：Sadly, in the twentieth century, the great port of Manham declined, and
thousands of workers were forced to
emigrate
out of the area
答案解析：题干上有twentith century的明显限定，根据这一时间可以定位到上面这句话。A选项中went away与emigrate out of the area同义替换，由此确定答案。
第15题答案：C
对应原文：It then brought in paid professionals to match installations with maps of the original port complex
and to set about reconstructing it
答案解析：A选项错在discover，港口的地址一直都很清楚，不用寻找。B选项完全没有提到。C选项rebuild与reconstructing同义替换，由此确定答案。
第16题答案：trains
对应原文：
We suggest you start with the visit to the
copper
mine.
Travel on
converted
mining
trains
and journey into the depths of the mountain along seams once worked by hundreds of miners
答案解析：听到copper mine意识到答案即将到来，不过这道题的空前后词在听力原文中都没有明显的对应，需要大家根据词性，或者通过题干事先判断空上应该填一种交通工具来锁定trains。
第17题答案：dark
对应原文：But please be warned that, like all mines, ours is very
dark
and dosed in and we do say that children under five and also dogs should NOT be taken into the mine.
答案解析：这道题同样没有明显的提示词，不过dark读的很重，句子结构与题干相比没有太大的变化，难度要比上一题小。
第18题答案：games
对应原文：While looking round the classrooms, take a special look at our display of
games
答案解析：听到classrooms意识到答案即将到来，exhibition与display同义替换，由此确定答案为games。
第19题答案：guided tour
对应原文：And it’s recommended that you time your visit to
coincide
with a
guided tour
答案解析：guided tour是雅思听力中经常考察的一个词组。因为吞音的问题，很容易听成guide tour，大家要稍微注意一下。
第20题答案：ladder/ladders
对应原文：Please take care going down the
ladders
if you wish to visit the lower deck—we don’t recommend you allow young children to use them.
答案解析：这道题采用语序颠倒的手法，先说答案，然后隔了一句话才说题干上的提示词。比较考验大家的短期记忆能力。
