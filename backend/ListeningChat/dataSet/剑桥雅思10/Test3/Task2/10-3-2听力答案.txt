剑桥雅思10 Test3 Section2听力答案解析
第11题答案：C
对应原文：The charity uses its money to support campaigns—for example, for changes in fishing policy and so forth
答案解析：C选项将原文中的uses its money to support第换成finance，其他基本没有变化，由此确定为正确答案。
第12题答案：E
对应原文： we now have volunteers working in
observation
, office work and other things
答案解析：E选项用in various ways概括了原文中observation, office work等内容，由此确定为正确答案。这道题不少同学可能会选D，其错误主要在于，选项中用的是一般现在时，即现在雇有海豚专家，而录音中则说的是it hopes，即希望雇佣，其实现在还没有，由此排除。
第13题答案：B
对应原文：
Although it’s not meant an enormous amount of money for us,
it has made our activities even more widely publicised and understood
.
In the long term it may not bring in extra members but we’re hoping it’ll have this effect.
答案解析：这道题三个选项在原文中都有对应的点。A选项extra money对应an enormous amount of money，但文章来自老烤鸭雅思前面有否定，因此排除。C选项more members对应extra members，但前面依然有否定，也排除。B选项中better known对应more widely publicised and understood，而且前面没有任何否定词，由此确定为答案。
第14题答案：A
对应原文：We’re campaigning against this because, although there’ll be little pollution from oil,
exploration creates a lot of underwater noise
答案解析：C选项完全没有提到，B选项勉强可以对应录音中的pollution from oil，但前面有little这一否定词，因此排除。A选项对应underwater noise，由此确定答案。
第15题答案：C
对应原文：
Then I came across this story about a family of dolphins who had to leave their home in the Moray Firth because of the oil companies and about a child who campaigned to save them.
I couldn’t put the book down—I was hooked
答案解析：原文中虽然提到了A选项和B选项，但前面都有否定，因此排除。只有C选项能够对应录音汇总couldn’t put the book down，由此确定答案。
第16题答案：B
对应原文：Unfortunately,
Echo is being rather
elusive
this year and hasn’t yet been sighted by our observers
答案解析：这5道匹配都不算太难，只要大家守好题干中的限定条件，不要被原文中的描述所干扰，就能比较容易的确定答案。16题seen与sighted同义替换，由此确定Echo为答案
第17题答案：C
对应原文：All the others have been out in force—Samson and Moondancer are often photographed together but
it is Kiwi who’s our real ‘character’ as she seems to love coming up close for the cameras
答案解析：虽然前面提到了Samson and Moondancer，但一方面它们两个是并列，要选的话得一起选，而题目只能填一个答案。另一方面，它们后面还有转折，需要关注but后的内容。由此排除Samson和Moondancer，确定C Kiwi为答案。
第18题答案：D
对应原文：They all have their own personalities— Moondancer is very elegant and curves out and into the water very smoothly,
whereas
Samson has a lot of energy
答案解析：energy与energetic同义替换，很容易确定Samson为答案。
第19题答案：D
对应原文：You’d probably expect him to be the youngest—he’s not quite—that’s Kiwi—but
Samson’s the latest of our dolphins to be chosen for the
scheme
答案解析：这道题主要考察同义替换。一些同学可能觉得yongest是最高级，跟题干中newest的意思有些相近，就将Kiwi确定为答案。但一方面Kiwi后面有but，另一方面latest明显更加对应newest的要求，由此确定D为答案，而不是C。
第20题答案：A
对应原文：Echo and Moondancer are noisy too, but
Moondancer’s easy to find because she has a particularly large
fin
on her back
答案解析：录音中将题干上的unusual shape具象化，变成particularly large fin，由此确定答案为Moondancer。
