剑桥雅思10 Test2 Section2听力答案解析
第11题答案：A
对应原文：As many of you may know,
the idea for these initiatives came from you, the public
, in the
extensive
consultation exercise which the City Council conducted last year
答案解析：这些观点来自于公众，A选项local people与the public同义替换，由此确定答案。B选项City Council去年举办的调查，而SWRDC则负责出钱，均不符合题干所问问题，因此统统排除。
第12题答案：C
对应原文：with up-to-the-minute features such as a recycling system -the only one of its kind in the world—
which enables seawater to be used in the pool
答案解析：这道题的解题关键在于题干中的unusual。录音中虽然说了很多文章来自老烤鸭雅思有关设计风格的问题，但找不到unusual的对应，因此排除A。C选项water treatment与seawater或者recycling system对应，而且还有the only one of its kind的形容（对应unusual），由此确定答案。
第13题答案：C
对应原文：Now,
there has been quite a bit of discussion in the local press about whether there would be enough room for the number of visitors we’re hoping to attract
答案解析：虽然原文中确实提到了opening date和项目花费的问题，但前者已经确定，而后者则是well within budget，均不符合题干中worries about的要求，因此统统排除。B选项size与enough room对应，而且这句中还提到了local press（对应local newspaper)，由此确定答案。
第14题答案：A
对应原文：
There’ll also be a competition for the public to decide on the
sculpture
we plan to have at the entrance
: you will decide which famous historical figure from the city we should have.
答案解析：开业时间和剪彩人员都已经确定，只有雕塑还没有定下来，由此确定答案为A。这道题中，statue和sculpture的同义替换在雅思听力中经常考察，大家可以积累一下。
第15题答案：E
对应原文： For example, there is a section on Asia, and
this is represented by rides and equipment in the shape of snakes, orangutans, tigers and so on
答案解析：听到Asia意识到答案即将到来。虽然orangutans（红毛猩猩）大多数同学都不认识，但我们仍然可以根据snakes和tigers确定答案为E。
第16题答案：F
对应原文：Moving south to the Antarctic—we couldn’t run to an ice rink I’m afraid but opted instead for
climbing blocks in the shape of mountains
答案解析：这道题设置了一个小的干扰项，先否定ice rink（对应C），然后再说答案mountains，由此锁定F。
第17题答案：D
对应原文：Then on to South America—and here the theme is El Dorado—
games replicating the search for mines full of precious Stone
答案解析：这道题的同义替换比较巧妙。需要大家联想到precious stone-宝贵的石头，就是宝石、珠宝的意思，从而确定D为答案。也有同学因为只听到了stone，觉得只有I跟它比较相似，进而选错答案。
第18题答案：H
对应原文：I know the contribution of cinema and jazz was considered but the designers finally opted for
rockets and the International Space Station
答案解析：同样是设置了小小的干扰项。上来先说cinema and jazz（对应G选项），然后转折接正确答案。哪怕大家一开始听到rockets没有反应过来，根据space station也可以确定H为答案。
第19题答案：A
对应原文：Eastwards to Europe then, and perhaps the most traditional choice of all the areas:
medieval
castles and other fortifications
答案解析：虽然不少同学都不认识forts，进而也没办法根据castle和fortification与forts的同义替换来确定A为答案。但我们还可以根据traditional和ancient来辅助做题。
第20题答案：B
对应原文：Then last, but not least, moving south to Africa and a whole set of wonderful mosaics and trails to represent the
great rivers
of this
fascinating
and varied continent.
答案解析：这道题的难点主要在于rivers读的比较轻，而且前面还有mosaics这样一个明显重读，但含义又不太清楚的词汇干扰我们的注意力。不过只要听到了rivers，答案就特别明显了。
