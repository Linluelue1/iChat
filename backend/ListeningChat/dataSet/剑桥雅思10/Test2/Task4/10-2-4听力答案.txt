剑桥雅思10 Test1 Section4听力答案解析
第31题答案：competition
对应原文：One area I want to mention is business markets, and I’m sure a really significant development will be a major increase in
competition
答案解析：听到business markets意识到答案即将到来，greater与increase同义替换，由此确定competition是答案。一些同学可能会因为significant与greater的对应，以为答案是development。但这个词填上去之后，并不符合后面among companies的含义，由此排除。
第32题答案：global
对应原文：
At the same time, mergers and takeovers mean that governments are actually losing power to major
global
corporations
答案解析：听到power意识到答案即将到来，corporations与companies同义替换，根据修饰关系确定global为答案。
第33题答案：demand
对应原文：A third point I want to make about markets is that in the rapidly expanding economies, such as India, China, Brazil and Russia,
demand
is growing very fast
答案解析：一些同学可能会将economies当作答案写上去。但economies在这里表示的是经济体，即后面提到的那些国家，而非经济。如果填上去的话就会跟题干后面的countries重复，由此排除。rising与growing对应，由此确定答案为demand。
第34题答案：customers
对应原文：
I think businesses are becoming more open to external influences.
In
particular
, companies are consulting
customers
more and more before making their business decisions.
答案解析：这道题其实就是考察discussion与consulting的同义替换，但由于小标题external influences距离答案太近，很容易反应不过来。这里老烤鸭小编要提醒大家的是，如果小标题下第一行出题，那么答案与小标题之间可能没有很长的过渡句，需要万分警惕。
第35题答案：regulation
对应原文：And in some cases,
regulation
will need to be strengthened
答案解析：这道题比较考验大家的耐心，需要一一排除lifecyles, pollution以及waste才能等到答案。lifecycles是因为不符合题干含义，而pollution和waste则是因为并列。
第36题答案：project
对应原文：I think we’re going to see a greater emphasis within companies on teams created with a specific
project
in mind
答案解析：听到teams意识到答案即将到来，specific与particular同义替换，由此确定答案为project。
第37题答案：flexible
对应原文：Typical examples that will attract and
retain
staff are traditional ones like
flexible
hours and—something that has been made possible by advances in technology—remote working
答案解析：这道题的难点在于没有明显的提示词，而且采用了语序颠倒的手法，不过hours读的很重的，根据修饰关系还是比较好确定flexible为答案。
第38题答案：leadership
对应原文：
Above all they’ll need great skills in
leadership
, so that their organisation can
initiate
and respond to change in a fast-moving world
答案解析：great与good同义替换，因此锁定skills和leadership这两个单词，其中skills填上去之后与题干含义不符，由此排除，确定leadership为答案。
第39题答案：women
对应原文：The
predominant
style of management will almost certainly become more
consultative
and
collaborative
, caused above all, by more
women
moving into senior management positions.
答案解析：这道题的难点主要在于断句。如果大家事先认为空与changes influenced by是一个意群，那么只能根据句义理解来确定答案，难度大大增加。如果认为空与taking senior roles是一个意群，那么听到senior management positions之后，就可以根据回忆确定women为答案。
第40题答案：self-employed
对应原文：Instead, more and more people are becoming
self-employed
, to gain the freedom and control over their lives that they’re unlikely to get from being employed.
答案解析：这道题比较简单，根据more and more可以很轻易的锁定self-employed，注意下单词拼写即可。
