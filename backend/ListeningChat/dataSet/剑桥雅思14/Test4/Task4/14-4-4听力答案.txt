剑桥雅思14 Test4 Section4听力答案解析
第31题答案：spring
对应原文：seven half-tonne stones standing in a semicircle around a
spring
, that might have been used for ceremonial purposes.
答案解析：听到semicircle和stones意识到答案即将到来，空前词几乎没有进行同义替换，根据修饰关系很容易确定spring（泉水）为答案。
第32题答案：tools
对应原文：Research on the buildings,
tools
and the human remains has revealed how the bustling village once functioned
答案解析：考察并列关系。听力原文故意对structures进行了同义替换，希望起到干扰作用。老烤鸭小编这里提示大家，遇到这种考察多项并列的情况，听节奏与停顿其实更容易一些。
第33题答案：maps
对应原文：Autonomous underwater vehicles, or AUVs, are used in the oil industry, for instance, to create
maps
of the seabed before rigs and pipelines are installed.
答案解析：听到oil industry和for instance意识到答案即将到来，空前词make与create同义替换，根据修饰关系确定maps为正确答案。
第34题答案：heavy
对应原文：Until relatively recently they were very expensive, and so
heavy
that they had to be launched from a large vessel with a winch.
答案解析：同样考察并列关系。听力原文没有设置任何障碍，听到expensive和and即可确定heavy为正确答案。
第35题答案：marble
对应原文： The site is the final resting place of an ancient Roman ship, which sank in the sixth century AD while ferrying prefabricated
marble
elements for the construction of an early church.
答案解析：从题干architectural elements可以推测出空上应该填一种老烤鸭雅思建筑材料。ancient Roman ship提示大家答案即将到来，根据事先预测的内容锁定marble（大理石）。
第36题答案：light
对应原文：For short distances, AUVs can share data using
light
, while acoustic waves are used to communicate over long distances.
答案解析：这道题采用语序颠倒的手法，将空后提示词放在答案之前，很容易确定light为正确答案。
第37题答案：camera(s)
对应原文：it can share the coordinates of the object – that is, its position – with a nearby AUV that carries superior
cameras
答案解析：这道题的难点主要在于better和superior的同义替换，只要能识别出来就挺容易的。
第38题答案：medical
对应原文：In 1974, a 2,000-year-old Roman vessel was discovered here, in 18 metres of water. When it sank, it was carrying
medical
goods
答案解析：空前词在原文中甚至都没有进行同义替换，只要能跟上节奏找到句子就很容易锁定medical。
第39题答案：eyes
对应原文： tablets that are thought to have been dissolved to form a cleansing liquid for the
eyes
答案解析：听到tablets意识到答案即将到来，clean与cleansing同义替换，再加上词性筛选，锁定eyes为正确答案。
第40题答案：wine
对应原文：Some were used for transporting cargoes of olive oil, and others held
wine
答案解析：再次考察并列。只要能听到olive oil就几乎不存在任何难度。
