剑桥雅思13 Test3 Section4听力答案解析
第31题答案：tongue(s)
对应原文：sleepy lizards are brown, but what’s particularly distinctive about them is the colour of their
tongue
答案解析：听到brown意识到答案即将到来，空前词blue与colour同义替换，由此确定答案为tongue。
第32题答案：plants
对应原文： They have quite a varied diet, including insects and even small animals, but they mostly eat
plants
of varying kinds.
答案解析：diet提示大家答案即将出现，从题干可以推测空上应该填它们吃的某种食物，insects和small animals并列，不可能作为答案出现，mostly与空前词mainly同义替换，由此锁定plants为正确答案。
第33题答案：snakes
对应原文： Large birds like cassowaries were one of the main ones in the past, but nowadays they’re more likely to be caught and killed by
snakes
答案解析：Section4中并列十分常见的考法，在birds和答案之间插入解释说明性的句子，注意诸如but，as well as, also等提示词就好。
第34题答案：sky
对应原文：they could usually find their way back home as long as they could see the
sky
– they didn’t need any other landmarks on the around.
答案解析：这道题没有明显的提示词，空前词的同义替换也不好找，只能根据词性和重读，再加上句义辅助来搜索答案。
第35题答案：partner(s)
对应原文：Unlike most animals, it seems that they’re relatively monogamous, returning to the same
partner
year after year
答案解析：听到observation意识到答案即将到来，空前词same原词出现，很容易锁定partner为答案。
第36题答案：contact
对应原文：But in fact observers have noted that once the babies have hatched out of their eggs, they have hardly any
contact
with their parents
答案解析：but提示答案即将到来，litter与hardly any同意替换，由此锁定contact。
第37题答案：protection
对应原文：So maybe the male lizards stay around so they can give the female lizards
protection
from other males
答案解析：听到段落开头的another意识到36题已经结束，录音进行到了37题。空前词provide与give同义替换，由此锁定答案为protection。
第38题答案：tail(s)
对应原文：So we caught some lizards in the wild and we developed a tiny GPS system that would allow us to track them, and we fixed this onto their
tails
答案解析：GPS system提示答案即将到来，从题干含义可以推测出空上填的应该是蜥蜴的某个部位，再加上所有格的同义替换，由此锁定tails。
第39题答案：steps
对应原文：we were able to track them for twelve days and gather data, not just about their location, but even about how many
steps
they took during this period.
答案解析：听到location意识到答案即将到来，空前词the number of与how many同义替换，根据修饰关系，确定答案为steps。
第40题答案：injury/injuries
对应原文：but one hypothesis is that male lizards can cause quite serious
injuries
to one another
答案解析：but可以提示大家后面说的才是重点内容，不过空前词在原文中并没有什么体现，只能根据重读和词性，再加上句义的辅助来判断答案。
